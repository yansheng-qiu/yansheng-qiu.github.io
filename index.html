I am a Research Scientist in the Graphics Team at CyberAgent AI Lab. I earned my PhD degree from the University of Tokyo in 2024, under the guidance of Shin'ichi Satoh. My doctoral studies were supported by the MEXT Scholarship and RIISE. Previously, I completed my Master's degree at National Taiwan University in 2020, where I worked with Yung-Yu Chuang.

My research road has been significantly shaped during my collaboration with Jia-Bin Huang, Yinqiang Zheng, Jian Wang, and Jinwei Gu. I also appreciate the internship with Shree Nayar's team at Snap Research, and Deep Generative Model team at SONY AI. Before my PhD study, I spent some time at the NII in Japan and NTU in Singapore. After then, I decided to live in Japan.

I am passionate about collaboration and mentorship and have actively worked with research groups led by Yinqiang Zheng, Yung-Yu Chuang, Zheng Wang, Yu-Lun Liu, and Kaipeng Zhang. Feel free to reach out to me if you are interested in collaboration or chatting anything.

Research

My research is centered on Computational Photography and Imaging, with a focus on capturing the intricacies of the physical world through innovative hardware and algorithmic designs. I am now keen on blending physical principles with generative models for reconstructions and interactions of the physical world.
