Zhixiang Wang (汪智祥)

I am a MEXT funded PhD student at the University of Tokyo with Shin'ichi Satoh starting from Oct. 2021. My research interest spans a broad range of topics in computational imaging, computational photography, and computer vision. I also work closely with Yinqiang Zheng and Jia-Bin Huang.

I completed my M.Phil at National Taiwan University with Yung-Yu Chuang. During my master's study, I was fortunate to work at National Institute of Informatics as a research intern. I have also spent some time at Nanyang Technological University in Singapore.
